About Citizen AI

Citizen AI is an intelligent platform designed to bridge the gap between citizens and government services through artificial intelligence.

Our mission is to make government interactions more accessible, efficient, and transparent by providing citizens with an AI-powered assistant that can help with questions, concerns, and civic engagement.

In Simple Terms:

  • Ask Questions: Get instant answers about government services, policies, and procedures
  • Report Issues: Easily report concerns and problems in your community
  • Get Help: Navigate complex government processes with AI assistance
  • Stay Informed: Access up-to-date information about civic matters
